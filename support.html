FAQ

Q: How long should I expect the delivery process to complete after purchasing?
A: Depending on the weather, traffic, and break condition, the delivery time varies from one afternoon to 5 days. Extreme conditions are not into considerations.

Q: Which architecture is your CPU based on? How could I program assembly codes for your CPU?
A: Our CPU is based on a newly developed architecture, and assembly codes are available on the documentation.

Q: Would your products be fully compatible with previously compiled x86 applications and the newest version of Windows?
A: Yes, our products are fully compatible with previously compiled x86 applications, and the newest version of Windows.

Q: Can I overclock my CPU?
A: Only the CPUs which has suffix “K” (C12 1090K) is promised to be overclockable. However, you could overclock the other CPUs at your own risk.

Q: How powerful is the integrated graphics card?
A: Varied by the suffix, the integrated GPU would have performance similar to RTX 3050 or RTX 3060.

Q: Would your motherboard be compatible with future CPUs?
A: Yes, our motherboard is promised to be compatible with at least 3 generations of CPU.
